Broccoli

Some of illustrations from Alexander Viazmensky's workshop

At the end of February 2015 Alexander Viazmensky, a remarkable artist from Saint Petersburg, performed his workshop in botanical painting in the space called “Perspective”. The main aim of the workshop was to learn to depict botany as realistically as possible.

At first we were drawing colored leaves and polypores. Then I wanted to challenge myself and chose something more difficult.

Broccoli is difficult enough for botanical painting. No one but Alexander believed that I would manage.

I wanted to portray broccoli more realistically. It is always useful to set such tasks. They develop mindfulness and patience.

Step by step, I got closer to the set target.
