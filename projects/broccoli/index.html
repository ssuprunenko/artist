Broccoli

Some of illustrations from Alexander Viazmensky's workshop

In the end of February 2015 a wonderful Saint Petersburg artist Alexander Viazmensky made botanical painting workshop in the "Perspectiva" space. The main goal was to learn as much as possible realistically to represent botany.

We began the workshop by drawing colored leaves and polypore, but then I wanted to challenge yourself and choose something a little more complicated.

Broccoli is quite complicated for botanical painting. Nobody but Alexander didn't believe that I can do it.

I would like to draw the most realistic broccoli. It is useful to set such challenges. They train mindfulness and patience.

Step by step I was close to goal.
